The subtyping expression "T1 <: T2" evaluates to "true" "T1" is a subtype of "T2".
The expression "T1 :> T2" evaluates to "true" "T2" is a subtype of "T1".
The expression "T1 == T2" evaluates to "true" "T1" is a subtype of "T2" and if "T2" is a subtype of "T1".
Subtyping expressions are used in subtyping constraints for generic types.
